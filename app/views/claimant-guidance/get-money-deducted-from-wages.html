Get money deducted from wages

You can ask the court to send an order to their employer ordering the employer to take money from their wages. This is often called an attachment of earnings order.

You can only:

  • claim your money back if they earn over the protected earnings rate
  • apply for an attachment of earnings order if you have their employer’s details

The court fee is £110.

Download and fill in a request for an attachment of earnings order form N337.
