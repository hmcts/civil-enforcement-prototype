What to do if you cannot pay

If you cannot pay any of your debt you can apply to get the court order changed. Download and fill in application form N244.

Get advice about debt

You can get advice specific to your situation from:

  • National Debtline
  • Money Advice Service
  • Citizens Advice
  • StepChange Debt Charity
