Set up a payment plan

If you cannot pay your debt in full you can pay in instalments, known as a payment plan.

Before you try to set up a payment plan you should first plan your budget. You need to make a repayment offer that you can afford otherwise you may miss payments. If you miss a payment more serious action may be taken against you, such as bailiffs coming to your home.

There are many online budget planners you can use for free, such as:

  • Money Advice Service budget planner
  • StepChange guidance on how to make a budget
  • National Debtline budget planner
  • Citizenâ€™s Advice budget planner

When you know what you can afford you should contact the person or business you owe money to and make an offer. Write a letter or email and include:

  • your budget
  • a list of your other debts
  • any illnesses or disabilities you have

StepChange have a free template letter on their website that you can download and use.
