If you cannot pay your debt

If you cannot pay any of your debt you can apply to get the court order changed. To do this, fill in the N244 application form

You’ll need to:

  • pay a £50 court fee unless you’re eligible for help with court fees
  • give details of your financial situation, including a statement of means

The court will schedule a hearing and send the time and date of your hearing in the post. At the hearing a judge will listen to your case and make a decision on your application.
