Overview

If you owe a person or business money and do not pay the debt or keep up with payments they may ask the court for help, such as:

  • sending a bailiff to your home or business
  • taking money from your wages
  • freezing money in your bank account
  • putting a charging order on property you own

Ask for time to manage your debts

You can ask claimants to stop contacting you for 30 days. This time is so you can:

  • get debt advice
  • work out how to manage your debts

Get advice from StepChange on how to ask for extra time.
