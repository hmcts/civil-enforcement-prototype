If you cannot keep up with payments

You can ask the court to change the amount you must pay every month. To do this, fill in the N245 application form.

You’ll need to:

  • pay a £50 court fee unless you’re eligible for help with court fees
  • give details of your financial situation
  • state how much you can afford to pay each month

The person or business you owe money to must agree to the new amount. If they do not, the court will decide on the amount you have to pay. The new amount might be higher than the amount you think is affordable. If you disagree with the decision you have 14 days to ask the courts to reconsider.
