Charge the debtor’s land or property

You can ask the court to charge the debtor’s land or property. If they sell any land or property, they must first pay their debt to you before they get their money.

The court will not put a charging order on property:

  • without a copy of the deed from the Land Registry
  • that is jointly owned

The court will first place an interim charging order on the property. After 3 weeks it becomes final unless the debtor disagrees with the order. If they disagree, a hearing will take place where a judge will look at the evidence before making a decision.

The court fee is £110.

Download and fill in a request for a charging order form N379.
