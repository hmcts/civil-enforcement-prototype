Pay your CCJ

Make a payment or pay the judgment in full.

Apply to vary your CCJ

You can ask the court to reduce the payments on the CCJ if you cannot afford the repayment amounts. The court will decide if changing the payment is fair to both you and {{ claimant }}.

You will need to give details of your income and spending and state how much you can afford to pay.

The court fee is £50. If you’re on a low income you might not have to pay the fee. Apply for help with fees.

Apply to change the payments on your CCJ

Apply to cancel your CCJ

Ask the court to cancel your CCJ if you do not owe the money. This is known as setting aside a judgment. You can apply to set aside the judgment if you did not receive, or did not respond to, the original claim from the court saying you owed the money.

The court fee is £255. If you’re on a low income you might not have to pay the fee. Apply for help with fees.
