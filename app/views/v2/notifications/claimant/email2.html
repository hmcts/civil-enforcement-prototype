Judgment registered - take court action to get your money back

Dear Sam Green,

You have successfully registered a county court judgment against Andrew Smith.

You can now apply to the court to collect the money for you. Depending on what information you have on Andrew Smith you can apply to:

  • send a bailiff to their home or business
  • take money from their wages
  • freeze money in their bank account
  • put a charging order on property they own

Find out more at https://www.gov.uk/resolve-unpaid-claim.

Regards, RUMC (Resolve an unpaid money claim)
