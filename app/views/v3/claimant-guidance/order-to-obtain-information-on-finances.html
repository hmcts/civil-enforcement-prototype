Get an Order to Obtain Information on finances

Ask the court to order the person or business who owes you money to attend court to give evidence of their finances, for example bills and statements.

The court fee is £55.

If a business owes you money, you can ask for an officer from the company to attend court and give details of its accounts.

It might be possible to set up a payment plan on the day. If not, you will have enough information to help you decide if and how you want the court to get your money back.
