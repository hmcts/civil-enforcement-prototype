Stop a Writ of Control

You can apply to stop a Writ of Control if:

  • you do not owe the money and want to cancel the county court judgment (CCJ) against you
  • your financial situation has changed and you want to vary the amount on the CCJ

You will need to give evidence to support your application. The court fee is £255. If you’re on a low income you might not have to pay the fee. Apply for help with fees.
