Before you apply

You are about to apply for a High Court bailiff to collect money from Andrew Smith using a Writ of Control.

  1. In the application form you will need to name the High Court bailiff or company you want to use.
  2. Before applying you should consider if Andrew Smith will be able to pay the debt right now. You may not be able to get your money back if Andrew Smith:
    • is a vulnerable person - has a physical disability or mental illness made worse by a bailiff visit
    • does not have an income
    • does not have any luxury items, such as a car
